# Talent Tier Rubric (quick reference)

Mirrors `memory/icp-talent.md`. Pool focus: ex-NovaLabs alumni.

## Hard filter (every tier)

Target only if **likely interested in Northwind** AND **not a current
Northwind investor/employee**. Fail → Tier-3 (not a sourcing target).

## Tier 1 — pursue (any one)

- Strong B2B or B2C experience built in a Tier-1 country.
- Strong technical base (deep eng/ML/research).
- Big Tech in a high position (Team Lead and up).
- Strong in AI media products (generative video/image, creative AI).

## Tier 2 — keep in pipeline (any one)

- Any Big Tech experience on Product or GTM.
- Any US startup experience.
- Has worked with AI media models (diffusion, video/image generation).

## Tier 3 — Trash

- None of the above, or fails the hard filter.

## Confidence

- **High**: clear structural signal (senior Big Tech role, strong technical base,
  or AI-media product work).
- **Medium**: strong B2B/B2C + Tier-1 country.
- **Low**: thin profile or Tier-2-only signals — verify before acting.

## Judgment notes

- "Likely interested": infer from trajectory (building in AI media, restless at a
  slow org, prior consumer/creator-AI work). Flag confidence; never assert.
- Don't upgrade on a company name alone — use evidence from the actual profile.
