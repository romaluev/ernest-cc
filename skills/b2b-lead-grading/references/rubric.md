# B2B Tier Rubric (quick reference)

Mirrors `memory/icp-b2b.md`. Primary ICP: AI studios, ad/creative agencies.

## Tier 1 — Alex sees / replies personally (any one)

- Fortune 500 or Forbes Global 2000.
- Clear market leader / large market share.
- AI studio or ad/creative agency (core ICP).
- Wants enterprise **now**: procurement, rollout, seats, MSA, contract, RFP.
- Top ~20 company in its country.
- Model provider or cloud provider.
- Mentions prior contact with Alex.
- Large fund (> $2B AUM).
- Strong media presence / high reputation person.

## Tier 2 — handle, no deep CEO time (any)

- Legitimate small company (not a market leader).
- Small fund (< $2B AUM).
- Genuine media enquiry.

## Trash — archive/decline, never surface (any)

- Cold vendor selling to us + small/unknown company.
- Small media (< ~100k readers).
- Generic mass pitch, SEO/backlink/guest-post spam.

## Decision order

1. CRM tier present? Use it.
2. Any Tier-1 signal (list, ICP category, enterprise intent, prior-Alex, big fund,
   reputation)? → Tier-1.
3. Clear trash signal and no Tier-1? → Trash.
4. Otherwise → Tier-2 (flag low confidence if unknown).

## Confidence

- **High**: CRM tier, or a list/category/provider/reputation/prior-contact hit.
- **Medium**: enterprise-intent keywords only.
- **Low**: pure inference / unknown company — must be human-verified before any
  Tier-1 treatment.
